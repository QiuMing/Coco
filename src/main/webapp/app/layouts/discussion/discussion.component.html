      Hello Jorge ,

      Welcome to the Flarum community. As much as we appreciate the contributions of everyone no matter who you are, there are some simple guidelines we follow to make this community a great place. The guidelines are linked in the menu above. But for simplicities sake, here are my thoughts on your contributions so far:

      • Use a short and precise title
      • Write content that clarifies the question you have, the more time you put into your effort, the more time others are willing to sacrifice their own to help you out.
      • Please try to move images meant for fun to our off-topic section, especially if you're having a question or topic you'd like others to contribute to.
